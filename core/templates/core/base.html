About Us

The NFC Student Management System is a Django-based web application developed to optimize student administration processes through role-specific functionalities for Registrars, Gatekeepers, Teachers, and Finance Staff. By integrating Near Field Communication (NFC) technology, the system facilitates efficient attendance tracking and comprehensive student profile management.
